To the wondrous world of jumping

Don't you ever feel the adrenaline when jumping at a large height?

Click to read more

Then this is the place for you! We take pride in our crazy activities, we plan our days on the best spots to perform the most extreme bungee jumping you can ever imagine.

Yes, we do safety checks

Although we like to make it extreme, we wouldn't want you to yeet yourself to oblivion as you shout out your ecstasy while plunging right into the ground.

A preview to our activities
  • Classic Jump

    The good old fall, scream, cry and happy bungee jumping.

  • Acrobatic Jump

    Show off your skills while doing poses while midair!

  • Beginner Mode

    Afraid of heights? No problem, we have tutorials made just for you!

  • Speed Jump

    A race between bungee jumper on who can dive down the fastest! A battle between speed.

  • Sky Shooters

    We have created a new activity where we can play nerf guns while jumping on midair, its extreme.

  • Galaxy Jump

    The top tier jump, the combination of all activities condense into one jump. Good luck.
